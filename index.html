Echoes of Athlone: A Musical Journey

Embark on a captivating exploration of Athlone's rich musical past. From the globally renowned orchestrations of Patrick Gilmore to the celebrated tenor Count John McCormack, and local stalwarts Patrick Keating, Michael Kilkelly, and Henry Foy, discover the stories that shaped our town's unique soundtrack.
